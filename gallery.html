Biodegradation of DEHP

Master's thesis investigating microbial degradation of Di-(2-ethylhexyl) phthalate (DEHP) using bacterial isolates and computational enzyme analysis.

  • Biodegradation studies
  • HPLC analysis

Microalgal Biotechnology

Current research at CSIR-CIMAP focuses on the valorisation of aromatic plant processing residues using microalgae and sustainable bioprocesses.

  • Biostimulant production
