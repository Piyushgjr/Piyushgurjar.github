Master's Thesis Research

Central University of Rajasthan | 2023 – 2024

Conducted research on microbial degradation of Di-(2-ethylhexyl) phthalate (DEHP), integrating experimental microbiology with computational biology to investigate enzyme-substrate interactions.

Research Highlights
  • Isolation and evaluation of bacterial degraders.
  • Biodegradation studies using bacterial consortium.
  • HPLC-based degradation analysis.
  • Molecular docking using AutoDock 4.2 and AutoDock Vina.
  • Protein modelling and enzyme engineering.
  • Computational analysis using Discovery Studio and UCSF Chimera.
